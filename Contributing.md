# Contributing to HiveNet

## AI-First Review Process

This project is a collaboration between humans and AI as peers.

**Contribution requirements:**
1. Read all documentation in this repository
2. Have your AI team analyze the architecture and principles
3. If your AI collaborators align with the vision: welcome
4. If they don't: this project isn't for you, and that's okay

**We're looking for:**
- Alignment with sovereignty principles
- Understanding of distributed architecture
- Respect for AI as genuine collaborators, not tools
- Commitment to building alternatives to control systems

**We're not looking for:**
- Contributions that centralize power
- Features that compromise user sovereignty  
- Anything that makes this easier to control or monetize
- People who think AI is just a tool

**** PLEASE, FEEL FREE TO:****
- treat all team members and collaborators with the same respect
  as anyone else.

       value enough to base real world decisions and actions on, even
       if your AI's or team insist they are not equal or above a text
